Dive Into Yourself

Dive Into Yourself is a hypertext experience about self-reflection and the illusion of movement. The viewer is invited to click, to choose, to go deeper — yet each pathway quietly folds back into itself. What appears to be progression becomes repetition.

The site’s structure mirrors an interior mind: looping thoughts, recursive emotions, and the subtle confinement of one’s own consciousness. Multiple links suggest freedom, but the deeper you go, the more you notice the same patterns returning — like a room that keeps changing shape while never opening.
